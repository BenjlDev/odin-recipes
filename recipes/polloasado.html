Pollo Asado

Main Ingredients:

  • 3-4 lbs Whole chicken

    • Marinade Ingredients:

    • Orange juice
    • Olive oil
    • Lime juice
    • White wine vinegar
    • Achiote powder
    • Ground coriander
    • Smoked paprika
    • Dried oregano
    • Ground cumin
    • Ground cloves
    • Minced garlic
    • Salt and black pepper.

      • Directions:

      1. Whisk together marinade ingredients

      2. Refrigerate and marinate the chicken in a large gallon size storage bag

      3. Seal the bag, mix everything together, and refrigerate 2-6 hours

      4. Remove the chicken from the bag and place it on a medium heat grill (350 degrees)

      5. Flip over to the other side for 15-20 Minutes

      6. Serve the chicken with a squeeze of lime juice
